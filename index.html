/õ-d-a/: dreaming of atmospheres

Atmospheres are something ephemeral and invisible, filling the spaces between the walls. There hides the essence of the place. I like to dream about them and turn it into substancia of my works.

O-D-A is an individual architecture studio, founded by architect Vika Pranaitytė and based in Kaunas, Lithuania. It’s usually one-man atelier, but each project gathers its own team around.
O-D-A focuses on practice, research and experimentation. The field of work is wide and includes: architectural – urban projects / interior design / exhibition architecture and graphic design / book design / architectural-art graphic / research and experimentation / architectural events.
